Heavy Duty

The heavy duty cycles use a long, hot wash along with a long, high speed tumble dry. This cycle is ideal for clothing with heavy amounts of soil, and is the best at removing stains and germs. However, hot water can shrink certain fabrics, so make sure to check before you wash.
